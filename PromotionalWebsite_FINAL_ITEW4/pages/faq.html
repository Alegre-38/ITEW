Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our travel services

Booking & Reservations

How far in advance should I book my trip?
We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for international trips, especially during peak seasons. For domestic travel, 1-2 months in advance is usually sufficient.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment plans are available for certain packages.
Can I make changes to my booking after it's confirmed?
Yes, changes can be made to your booking, but they may be subject to fees depending on the timing and type of change. Please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Travel Packages

What is included in your travel packages?
Our packages typically include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, and some meals. Specific inclusions vary by package, so please check the detailed itinerary for each trip.
Can I customize a package to suit my preferences?
Yes, we offer customizable packages. Our travel experts can help you create a personalized itinerary based on your interests, budget, and travel dates.
Are flights included in the package price?
International flights are not included in the base package price, but we can arrange them for you. Domestic flights within the destination country are usually included.

Travel Requirements

What documents do I need for international travel?
You'll need a valid passport (with at least 6 months validity), visas (if required), and any necessary vaccination certificates. We'll provide detailed information based on your destination.
Do I need travel insurance?
While not mandatory, we strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect against unexpected events such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, or lost luggage.
What vaccinations do I need?
Vaccination requirements vary by destination. We recommend consulting with a travel health specialist at least 6-8 weeks before your trip.

Customer Support

How can I contact customer support during my trip?
We provide 24/7 emergency support through our hotline. You'll receive contact information for local representatives in your destination before your trip.
What is your cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package and timing. Please refer to our Cancellation Policy page for detailed information.
Do you offer group discounts?
Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 10 or more people. Contact our group travel specialists for customized quotes and arrangements.
