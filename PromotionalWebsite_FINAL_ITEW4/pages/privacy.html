Your privacy is important to us. Read our policy here.

Privacy Policy Overview

At Wanderlust Travels, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment information (credit card details)
  • Travel preferences and history

How We Use Your Information

  • To process bookings and transactions
  • To improve customer service and support
  • To send periodic emails regarding your order or other products and services

Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your information is stored in secure networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
