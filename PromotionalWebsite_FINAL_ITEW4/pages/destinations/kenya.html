Kenya

The Heart of African Safari Adventure

Discover Kenya

Kenya is a land of breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and rich cultural heritage. Home to the famous Maasai Mara National Reserve and the Great Migration, Kenya offers some of the most spectacular safari experiences in Africa.

From the snow-capped peaks of Mount Kenya to the pristine beaches of the Indian Ocean, and from the vast savannahs to the bustling city of Nairobi, Kenya promises unforgettable adventures for nature lovers and culture enthusiasts alike.

Best Time to Visit

The best time for wildlife viewing is during the dry seasons: January to March and July to October. The Great Migration typically occurs between July and October.

Average Temperature: 20-30°C (68-86°F)

Must-See Attractions

Maasai Mara Safari

Experience the world-famous Great Migration and witness the Big Five in their natural habitat at this iconic wildlife reserve.

Diani Beach

Relax on pristine white-sand beaches and enjoy water sports along Kenya's beautiful Indian Ocean coastline.

Maasai Culture

Immerse yourself in the rich traditions of the Maasai people, known for their distinctive customs and vibrant attire.

Safari Tips

  • Book safaris with reputable operators
  • Pack neutral-colored clothing
  • Bring binoculars and a good camera
  • Respect wildlife viewing distances
  • Stay hydrated and use sunscreen

Local Cuisine

Kenyan cuisine is a delicious blend of African, Indian, and European influences. Must-try dishes include:

  • Nyama Choma (grilled meat)
  • Ugali (maize meal)
  • Sukuma Wiki (collard greens)
  • Chapati (flatbread)
  • Fresh tropical fruits
