Welcome to Grand Palais

Nestled in the heart of Paris, just steps away from the iconic Eiffel Tower, Grand Palais Hotel embodies the essence of French luxury and sophistication. Our historic building, dating back to the 19th century, has been meticulously restored to offer modern comforts while preserving its classical Parisian charm.

With breathtaking views of the Eiffel Tower, elegant interiors, and world-class service, Grand Palais Hotel provides an unforgettable experience in the City of Light.
