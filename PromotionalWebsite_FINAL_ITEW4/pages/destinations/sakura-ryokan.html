Welcome to Sakura Ryokan

Experience the serene beauty and timeless traditions of Japan at Sakura Ryokan, nestled in the historic city of Kyoto. Our ryokan offers a harmonious blend of classic Japanese architecture, tranquil gardens, and modern comforts, providing guests with an authentic and peaceful retreat.

Enjoy the art of Japanese hospitality (omotenashi), savor exquisite kaiseki cuisine, and relax in our private onsen baths surrounded by cherry blossoms.
