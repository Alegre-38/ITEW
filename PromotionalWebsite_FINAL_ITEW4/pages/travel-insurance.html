Frequently Asked Questions

It's best to purchase travel insurance as soon as you book your trip. This ensures you're covered for any unexpected events that might occur before your departure, such as trip cancellation.

Common exclusions include pre-existing medical conditions (unless declared), extreme sports (unless specified), and losses due to war or civil unrest. Please review the policy details for complete information.

Claims can be made online through our portal or by contacting our 24/7 claims hotline. You'll need to provide documentation such as receipts, medical reports, or police reports depending on the type of claim.
