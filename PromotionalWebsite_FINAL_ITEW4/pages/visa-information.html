Visa Application Process

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Completed application form
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Travel itinerary

Frequently Asked Questions

It's recommended to apply for a visa at least 4-6 weeks before your planned departure date. This allows sufficient time for processing and any potential delays.

A visa on arrival allows you to obtain a visa when you arrive at your destination country. You'll need to present required documents and pay the visa fee at the airport or border crossing.

Visa extension policies vary by country. Some countries allow extensions through their immigration offices, while others require you to leave and re-enter. Check the specific requirements for your destination.
